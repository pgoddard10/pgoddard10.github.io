Who Can Join? People who are partially sighted (sight-impaired).

When & Where? Sessions by appointment, April to September 2025, at Bristol Museum and Art Gallery. Each session takes up to three hours.

What Happens on the Day?
On the day, we will meet you at the reception desk and take you to a private room in the museum, where we will run through the relevant paperwork with you and test your eyes. There are two eye tests that require you to read letters from a chart, and a field of vision test where you state when you can see the end of a pen that is moved around. This is so we can relate your feedback to the condition of your eyesight. You will receive a demonstration of the headset, then go into the art gallery with a researcher to try out some paintings. Whilst there, we'll go through each of the visualisations to see what you find helpful and what is not so helpful. The visualisations are magnification, contrast enhancement, sharpness boost, edge outlines, and a digital image overlaid on the physical art.
